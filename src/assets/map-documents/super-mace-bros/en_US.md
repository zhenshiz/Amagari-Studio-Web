## About the Map

Super Mace Bros is a vanilla PVP minigame for 2–8 players. Everyone fights with a mace, but it only deals damage when you fall and land a smash on an opponent.

Take the high ground or use the double jump to create enough height for a smash. Each successful mace hit increases the knockback dealt to the target, while items and mace modifiers around the arena can help you gain an advantage. The last player standing wins.

## Playing Tips

1. The map supports 2–8 players and requires Minecraft 26.1.2.
2. This is a community localization. Download the original map from [Minecraft Maps](https://www.minecraftmaps.com/52298-super-mace-bros), then download the localization resource pack separately.
3. Get the resource pack from [Lanzou Cloud](https://wwals.lanzn.com/iaGdJ3o25jti) using the password `amagar`, then follow the [localization installation guide](/install).
4. Drop from high ground or use the double jump to land smashes, and make good use of arena items and mace modifiers.
