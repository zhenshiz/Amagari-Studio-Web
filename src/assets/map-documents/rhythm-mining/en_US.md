## About the Map

Rhythm Mining is a single-player music map that combines mining with rhythm-game mechanics. Mine blocks in time with the music, build combos and score, and work your way through a distinctive rhythm challenge inside the mine.

The map includes songs ranging from beginner-friendly tracks to high-difficulty challenges, accompanied by visual feedback for combos, scores, and more. Its timing system has 4 judgment levels, and the judgment timing can be adjusted in 5-millisecond increments to suit different devices and player preferences.

## Playing Tips

1. This is a single-player map for Minecraft Java Edition 1.21.11.
2. The localization is integrated into the official map release. Download the complete map directly from [Minecraft Maps](https://www.minecraftmaps.com/52507-rhythm-mining); no separate localization resource pack is required.
3. Mine blocks to the beat and use the combo and judgment feedback to track your timing.
4. If your inputs feel out of sync with the music, adjust the judgment timing in 5-millisecond increments using the in-map settings.
5. Music featured in the map is provided by [BGMer](https://bgmer.net/).
