## About the Map

Business Battle is a two-player business competition map. Each player runs a shop in the same commercial district, producing goods, finding buyers, and building up funds. The first player to reach the target amount of money wins.

## Map Information

- Original creators: Rastyanutiy and monttii23
- Game version: Minecraft 26.1.2
- Players: 2
- Loader: Vanilla
- Localization: Official Chinese localization

## Core Gameplay

1. Check recipes in your shop and use the crafting station to make different goods.
2. Sell finished products to build up your funds.
3. Plan production, purchasing, and other tasks around waiting and business phases.
4. Inspect shops in the district for contraband; finding prohibited goods triggers a fine.
5. Explore other stores and use their facilities to exchange for or replenish production supplies.
6. Compare both players' funds and be the first to reach the target amount to win.
