## About the Map

The Dark Ship is a horror escape and puzzle map created by zannLeft. Players board a dark, abandoned ocean liner, explore its decks, solve puzzles, uncover what happened to the passengers, and try to escape before the darkness beneath the sea finds them.

The map takes about one hour and supports both single-player and multiplayer. It uses custom sounds, textures, models, and atmospheric effects, so the included resource pack is required.

## Installation and Play

1. Download the map from its [Minecraft Maps page](https://www.minecraftmaps.com/52345-the-dark-ship).
2. The downloaded outer archive contains both the world save and the required resource-pack archive.
3. Follow the [installation guide](/install) to place the world and resource pack, then enable the pack before entering the world so the custom sounds, textures, and models load correctly.
4. The map can be played alone or with others. Watch the environment for clues and avoid the walkthrough if you want to solve every puzzle yourself.
