## About the Map

Funkalot is a rhythm map that brings five-key falling-note gameplay into Minecraft. Players follow the music through multiple tracks and stages, timing their inputs to the beat.

## Map Information

- Original creator: TEHEK
- Game version: Minecraft 1.21.8
- Loader: Vanilla
- Localization: Official Chinese localization

## Playing Tips

1. Launch the map with Minecraft 1.21.8.
2. Make sure the original map resource pack is loaded, then place the Chinese localization pack above it.
3. If you record a video using this map, credit the original creator, TEHEK, in the description or a pinned comment.
